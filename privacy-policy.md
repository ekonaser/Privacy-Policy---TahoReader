## 1. Introduction
TahoReader is a mobile application designed for viewing and managing tachograph card data. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information and ensures compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). By using TahoReader, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
- **No Personal Data Collection:** The App does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users.  
- **No Analytics or Tracking:** The App does not use analytics, tracking tools, or advertising identifiers.  
- **No Emails or Newsletters:** The App does not send promotional emails or newsletters.  
- **No Payments:** The App does not process or accept any payments.  
- **No Advertising:** The App does not display ads or use retargeting technologies.

---

## 3. Permissions and Technologies
TahoReader uses limited device permissions strictly for core functionality:
- **Storage Access:** To read `.ddd` files and tachograph data stored on the device.  
- **Internet Access:** To enable optional data retrieval or updates.  
- **USB/OTG Access:** To read tachograph card data via compatible USB or OTG readers.

These permissions are used solely for the App’s intended purpose and never for data collection or tracking.
